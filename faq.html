FAQ

Here are some common questions about our community.

What are the main goals of the community?

The main goal of our community is to bring together students with interest and knowledge in the IT field.

How can I be notified of events as soon as possible?

You can follow us on social media by using the Instagram, WhatsApp and other icons in the footer, so you can learn about new events as soon as they are announced.

Who can join the IOSTREAM Community?

Our community is open to motivated high school and university students who are interested in programming, olympiads, startups or technology in general. You do not need to be a medalist or expert to join ‚Äì curiosity and willingness to learn are enough.

How can I apply for future camps or projects?

When we launch a new project, we share the application link on our Instagram page, WhatsApp channel and Linktree. You can follow those platforms, fill out the online application form, and we will contact you by email or WhatsApp with the next steps.
