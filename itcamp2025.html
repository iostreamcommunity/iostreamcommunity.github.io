Together with low-income students interested in computer science and IT, we launched the first project of our community, IT CAMP 2025. Throughout the camp, we worked with students on many different topics and question types. At the end, the top 3 students were selected as winners based on the final exam, class activity, and homework scores. All students were presented with certificates and gifts. Don't forget to follow our Instagram page and join our WhatsApp channel to stay informed about future projects!
